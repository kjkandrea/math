## 정비례(Direct)와 반비례(Inverse)

### 정비례(Direct)

`y = kx`

```
y = 1x
y = 2x
y = 1/2x
y = -2x
y = -1/2x
y = πx
y = -πx
y = nx
```

### 반비례(Inverse)
